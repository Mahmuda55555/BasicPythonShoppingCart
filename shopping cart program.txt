Shopping Cart Program:

1)Adding Items:
-users can add items by entering the name and price
-the items will be saved on the cart





2. Viewing the Cart:

-Users can view all the items in the cart, including the name and price of each item.
-The total cost of all items will also be displayed.



3. Remove Items:

-Users can remove a specific item from the cart based on its index.

4. Save and Load Cart:

-The cart will automatically load any previously saved data when the program starts.
-Users can save the cart to a text file before exiting.

5. OOP Structure:


-It consists of two main classes:

-Item class: Represents individual items with a name and price.
-ShoppingCart class: Manages the cart's operations (like adding, removing items, calculating total cost).
